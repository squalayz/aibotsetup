# CLAWD — Complete Color Scheme Overhaul

## THE NEW DIRECTION
Strip away ALL the typical AI startup colors (cyan, purple, green gradients). The new scheme is **Deep Navy & White/Silver** — ultra-clean, minimal, Apple-level sophistication. The ONLY color on the entire page should be the blue robot itself. Everything else is navy, white, and silver. This makes the robot the undeniable focal point and makes the site look nothing like every other AI company.

Think: **Apple's aesthetic meets a high-end consulting firm.** Confidence through restraint.

---

## NEW COLOR PALETTE — Apply Globally

Replace EVERY instance of the old colors across the entire codebase:

### Backgrounds:
- **Page background:** `#0a0f1e` (deep navy, NOT pure black — has warmth and depth)
- **Card backgrounds:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03)` (barely-there frosted panels)
- **Card hover:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.06)`
- **Section alternating bg (optional):** `#0d1224` (slightly lighter navy for contrast between sections)

### Text:
- **Headlines:** `#ffffff` (pure white — crisp, confident)
- **Body text:** `#a0aec0` (silver/cool gray — readable but soft)
- **Muted/tertiary text:** `#64748b` (slate gray)
- **NO colored text** — no cyan headlines, no purple gradients on text, no green text. ALL text is white or silver.

### Accents (used VERY sparingly):
- **Primary accent:** `#ffffff` (white — yes, white IS the accent color. Borders, buttons, lines.)
- **Secondary accent:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)` (for subtle borders, dividers, hover states)
- **The ONLY non-white/silver color allowed:** The blue glow from the robot (which comes from the image itself, not from CSS)

### What to REMOVE:
- ❌ All cyan (#00e5ff) — gone everywhere
- ❌ All purple (#a855f7) — gone everywhere  
- ❌ All green (#22c55e) — gone everywhere (including checkmarks, badges, dots)
- ❌ All gradient text effects — replace with solid white
- ❌ All colored glows and shadows — replace with white or remove
- ❌ Colored border animations — replace with white/silver
