The star of the show is a large, interactive AI robot/entity that lives in the background, watches the user's cursor, and reacts to their presence. Technical Approach:

Use Canvas 2D or SVG with GSAP for the face rendering and animations
The face should be drawn with connected points/vertices that form the head shape, eyes, and minimal features

Keep the face monochromatic (cyan/teal wireframe on dark background)
